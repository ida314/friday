What problem does this solve?
- This is trying to solve the problem of having to manually search for companies and job roles that fit your specific criteria. Current filters dont capture your company/job preferences nearly well enough, and is not done automatically. By making it automatic, it both saves time and allows the system to catch companies and jobs that you might have otherwise have missed. This will save you time, allowing for better use of your time, surface companies that you would have otherwise been unaware of, and give you an advantage in the hiring market as the system discovers companies and jobs that are not present on the main big career boards. We will complish this by using a mix of diverse company descovery resources, which are stored on a graph which we can traverse to find relationships between companies/projects/technologies/industries/ext..  

Who is the user?
- Myself
- College students looking for internships
- Newgrads
- Mid level employees looking to career change
- People wanting to save time and gain an advantage in the job hunt

What does a “good result” look like?
- a daily notification each morning with new jobs and/or companies, with a short explanation on why it matches your interests, with direct quotes pulled from the job posting or company site
- an optional dashboard where you can view all tracked companies, their job sites, as well as job boards
- an ability to manually seed companies or jobs through the dashboard

What should the daily/weekly output contain?
- daily output should contain new jobs that have been posted within 24 hours (job scrape 1x per day by default)
- weekly output should contain new companies that have been descovered and why they match the users interests (company search 1x per week by default)

What is explicitly out of scope?
- NOT a automatic company apply tool, this tool is explicitly made to push back against that notion of "shotgun" or "spray and pray" applying strategy. This tool encourages more careful applications to companies that more explisitly match your criteria and expriences/skills
